HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to hangman. This will be a short paragraph describing you how to play

The word to guess is represented by a row of dashes representing each letter of the word.

If you click a letter which occurs in the word to be guessed, the computer writes it in all its correct positions.

If the selected letter does not appear in the word, the computer creates a tally mark by drawing one element of a hung stick figure.

If the word is correct, the game is over and you win. Otherwise, the computer may choose to penalize the guesser by adding an element to the diagram.

On the other hand, you lose if you make enough inaccurate guesses to allow the computer to finish the diagram.

HAVE FUN!
